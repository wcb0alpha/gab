Grayson Anderson-Brown is a British-born Jamaican author and poet, a South London native, and a graduate of the City London University Novel Writing course.

With over 20 years of writing credits, he has been recognized by the "Spread The Word" emerging writers program and the London Library program. Most recently, he was named the winner of the Book Edit Writers' Prize 2025.

The Mountain Top
"It’s never easy to reach the mountain top
You begin with hopes and dreams
The endless possibilities
You judge other people’s success
Believing that you too can contest
Confidence and arrogance
Become one and the same
Imagine trying for the impossible
Without yet knowing the game"
